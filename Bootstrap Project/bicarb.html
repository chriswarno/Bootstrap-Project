Sodium bicarbonate, commonly known as baking soda is a widely used supplement in endurance sports. It is readily avaiable for most people, as baking soda is common to have, and it is in just about every grocery store. The optimal time to take sodium bicarbonate is 1.5 to 2 hours before high intensity training or competition. Sodium bicarbonate can improve an athlete's power, speed, work capacity, and time to failure during high intensity exercise.
