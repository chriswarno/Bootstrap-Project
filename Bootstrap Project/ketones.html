Ketones are a relatively newer supplement that has gained popularity in endurance sports in recent years. Ketones are a produced naturally in the liver as an energy source for muscles. Supplemental ketones are used to give the body a fuel source other than glycogen to use during low intensity exercise. The thought process behind this is that the body will save glycogen for higher intensity efforts such as a sprint. Since ketones are a newer supplement, there are limited studies on the efficacy of them.
